Kev Bayliss — Artist & Game Creator

I’m a British artist and game creator with a career spanning traditional painting, character design, and video game development. My journey began in the late 1980s at **Rare Ltd**, where I played a pivotal role in shaping some of the most beloved characters and franchises in video game history.

One of my most significant achievements was redesigning **Donkey Kong** for Nintendo, a project that led to a lasting collaboration between **Rare** and Nintendo. In addition, I co-created iconic character franchises such as **Battletoads**, **Diddy Kong**, and **Killer Instinct**, bringing to life unforgettable characters that have stood the test of time in the gaming world.

Throughout my career, I have worked on a variety of groundbreaking titles, contributing to the visual direction and character design of many games that helped define the golden age of the 16-bit and early 3D eras. My expertise spans hand-painted techniques and digital art, allowing me to create art that resonates with both gaming fans and art collectors alike.

Today, I focus on creating original paintings, game-inspired artwork, and commissioned pieces that reflect my love for storytelling and character design. Whether painting live on **Twitch** or working on custom commissions, my goal is to bring the same passion and creativity to each new project that I’ve poured into the games that shaped my career.
