Kev Bayliss — Artist & Game Creator

I’m a British artist and game creator with a career spanning traditional painting, character design, and video game development. Starting my professional journey in the late 1980s, I contributed to some of the most iconic games of the 16-bit and early 3D era. As a key member of the team at Rare, I had the privilege of bringing characters like Donkey Kong, Diddy Kong, and the Battletoads to life, leaving a lasting impact on the gaming world.

From the early days of creating the Battletoads and Donkey Kong characters, to later working on titles like **Killer Instinct** and **Starfox Adventures**, my work has spanned multiple generations of gaming. I’ve been fortunate enough to work with some of the most talented teams in the industry, creating characters that have become synonymous with the history of video games.

After my time at Rare, I continued to work in game art, transitioning to new projects and roles, including collaborations with **Playtonic Games** on the **Yooka-Laylee** franchise. My focus now lies in creating original paintings, game-inspired artwork, and custom commissions. I draw from my decades of experience, combining hand-painted techniques with my love for character design and storytelling.

Whether I’m creating a new piece of artwork or sharing my creative process live on **Twitch**, my goal is to bring the magic of game art and design to fans around the world. Join me on my journey as I continue to create and share my passion for video games and art.
