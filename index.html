Kev Bayliss — Artist & Game Creator

I’m a British artist and game creator with a career spanning traditional painting, character design, and video game development. I began my professional journey in the late 1980s, contributing to some of the most recognisable games of the 16-bit and early 3D era, and I continue to create new work today.

My current focus is original paintings, game-inspired artwork, and commissioned pieces, blending hand-painted techniques with decades of experience in digital art and character creation.

I regularly paint live on Twitch, sharing my process, techniques, and stories from a lifetime in game development.
