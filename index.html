Kev Bayliss — Artist & Game Creator

I’m a British artist and game creator with a career spanning almost 40 years in the video game industry. I began my journey in the late 1980s at Rare Ltd where I had the privilege of contributing to some of the most iconic titles of the 16-bit and early 3D era.

In 1994, I had the opportunity to redesign Donkey Kong, and I’m proud that this character has remained an iconic figure ever since. Alongside this, I created Diddy Kong for Nintendo and also the characters from Killer Instinct and Battletoads. These franchises have shaped the world of gaming, and I’m honored to have been part of that journey.

Throughout my career, I’ve worked on a variety of titles, contributing both to the visuals and gameplay mechanics that defined a generation of gamers. My work helped shape the golden era of gaming, and I have had the pleasure of collaborating with some of the most talented minds in the industry.

Today, I continue to create and share my passion for art and character design for video games. My focus is on original paintings, digital artwork, and commissioned pieces, blending hand-painted techniques with my experience in digital design. Whether I'm working on a new commission or painting live on Twitch, I’m always eager to share my creative process and the stories behind the characters and worlds I’ve helped bring to life.
