Kev Bayliss

Artist & Game Creator

I’m an artist and game creator with a long history of designing and bringing characters, worlds, and ideas to life for video games and beyond.

I’m perhaps best known for my work on DONKEY KONG COUNTRY and other classic video games, where I helped create and define characters that went on to become part of gaming history. My work spans character design, animation, and visual development across both games and traditional art.

Alongside my work in games, I create original traditional artwork, including paintings and one-off commissions.

My focus is on character, personality, and storytelling — whether that’s through digital creation or paint on canvas.

Selected original artworks are available to buy online.
